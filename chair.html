E-Novate R1MK-1

The standard design of our product is color black as it recommended for maintenance purposes, but we also have color variants which will provide the customer with a sense of customizability. Giving our buyers different options to choose from will allow them to be able to select what they actually want, rather than what is available. These variants also promote vibrance in the working environment, which induces positive psychological effects.

The structural design and materials used for E-novate’s products were heavily influenced by ergonomics and the criticism of the consumers of their current office chairs. The layout of the chair was designed in a way which ensures that the customer would feel comfortable while maintaining proper sitting posture to avoid any health complications such as back pain and spine dysfunction. The company also weighed the pros and cons of each material which are used in 25 commercial chairs, and ultimately decided to use vinyl fabric as it is cost-effective and is easy to clean and maintain.

Contains the following features which can be accessed through the control panel:

  • Speakers
  • Reclinability
  • Bluetooth Connection
  • Massage
