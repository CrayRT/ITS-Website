Emergencyard

Introducing the Emergencyard! Introducing the Emergencyard - the ultimate safety accessory for anyone on the go. Made from durable, high-quality materials, this lanyard is designed to be worn around your neck and features a built-in flashlight, whistle, or reflectors. You can also get any two combinations or all three! In case of an emergency, you can use the flashlight to signal for help, the whistle to attract attention, and the reflectors to increase your visibility. The lanyard also features an ID card holder, where you can store important information such as your name, emergency contact information, and any medical conditions. This makes it easy for first responders to quickly identify you and provide appropriate assistance. This lanyard is perfect for students, employees, and anyone who needs to keep their ID and emergency information within reach at all times. Its compact and lightweight design makes it easy to carry with you wherever you go, so you'll always have it when you need it. Don't take any chances when it comes to your safety. Get the Emergency Lanyard today and enjoy peace of mind knowing that you're prepared for any emergency situation.
